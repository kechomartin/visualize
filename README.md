# 🖼️ VangoVirtual

**VangoVirtual** is an immersive and interactive virtual online gallery that showcases curated collections of digital art, classic masterpieces, and user-submitted creations. Designed to bring the museum experience online, VangoVirtual makes art more accessible, engaging, and personal — anytime, anywhere.

## 🎨 Features

- 🖼️ **Virtual Art Gallery** – Seamlessly browse artworks in a clean, scrollable or immersive 3D environment.
- 🧑‍🎨 **Artist Profiles** – Learn more about the creators behind the art.
- 🔍 **Smart Search & Filters** – Find artworks by title, artist, category, or time period.
- 🗂️ **Curated Collections** – Explore themed exhibitions and featured artists.
- 💬 **Comments & Reactions** – Interact with the art through visitor feedback and discussion.
- 🧠 **AI-Powered Recommendations** *(optional)* – Get suggestions based on your viewing habits.
- 📱 **Responsive Design** – Enjoy the gallery experience on mobile, tablet, and desktop.
